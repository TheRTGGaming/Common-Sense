# Common Sense — Official Wiki

Welcome to the official documentation for **Common Sense**, a Minecraft mod created by The RTG Gaming (Richard).  
This wiki serves as a central hub for all mechanics, items, blocks, recipes, and systems introduced by the mod.

Common Sense focuses on:
- Adding realistic, intuitive mechanics that feel like natural extensions of vanilla Minecraft  
- Improving immersion through logical crafting, survival enhancements, and world interactions  
- Maintaining a clean, museum‑style design philosophy where every feature has purpose and clarity
